Numismatic Knowledge Base

Key Date

The rarest or most highly prized date and mint mark combination in a specific coin series.

Manufacturing Slang

**Broadstrike**: Struck without a collar, causing flattening. **Cud**: A raised area from a broken minting die. **Hairlines**: Tiny, thin scratches from improper handling.

Collector Slang

**Slab**: Certified and encapsulated coin. **Body Bag**: A coin returned due to damage or being counterfeit. **Cull**: A heavily worn, low-value coin. **Whizzing**: Illegal mechanical polishing.

Toning Spectrum

Natural discoloration from oxidation and sulfur. Desirable toning includes vibrant rainbow patterns, while undesirable toning appears dark or splotchy.

Natural vs. Artificial

Artificial toning is an intentional color alteration that often looks like "floating" paint or aggressive "bullseye" patterns.
